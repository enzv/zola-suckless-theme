Some themes ship math support. This one keeps the sample plain.
If you need math, keep it readable and local.

If you need formulas, you can still show them as text:

`a^2 + b^2 = c^2`

Display math can stay readable even when it is not rendered by a math engine:

`(a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2`

That is enough to show the spacing and rhythm without dragging in a full math stack.
